About Us

Miracle Fencing Club is a facility dedicated to the sport of sabre fencing. The club is opened by former Chinese national fencing team member Ji (Miracle) Qi, who participated in many national and international competitions. She coaches in both collegiate level and individual level. The club welcomes people with all levels of experience.
